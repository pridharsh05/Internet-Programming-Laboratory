Raisins

Raisins (Kismis) Next on our dry fruits list is Raisins, also known as Kishmish in India. This naturally sweet dry fruit is a good source of sugars, fat, protein, dietary fiber, various vitamins, and minerals.
Benefits:
Helps regulate blood pressure.
Aids in better digestion.
